In the second act, Grandpa travels to New York City on the train, and meets a woman named Elsa Tobin. They enjoy talking to each other.
Elsa: You said you have family in New York.
Grandpa: Yes, indeed.
Grandpa is looking forward to seeing his family. But does he want to stay with them permanently?
Elsa: Are you going to live with them?
Grandpa: Yes.
Elsa: Permanently?
