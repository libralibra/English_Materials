Unit 50: You'll be ill

1. I see a good future for you.

(There's a fortune-teller. He says he can see the future in his crystal ball.)

Mrs. Crystal If it's fine tomorrow, I'll go and see Elsie in the afternoon.
Dr. CrystalAll right, Doris.
Mrs. CrystalIf you eat too much, you'll be ill.
Dr. CrystalNo. Not me.
Mrs. CrystalYou've got a customer.
Dr. CrystalRight. It's a hard life. Welcome ... 
Girl Oh!
Dr. Crystal ... my child.
GirlHello.
Dr. CrystalSit.
GirlThank you.
Dr. CrystalSpeak.
GirlPardon?
Dr. CrystalSpeak. Tell me what you want to know.
GirlOh, well it's about Jason, my boyfriend.
Dr. CrystalI see him, yes.
GirlOh, do you? Well, I want to know— are we going to get married, Jason and me, that is?
Dr. CrystalTell me—when are you going to see him again? Will you see him tomorrow?
GirlYes. I'll see him in the evening.
Dr. CrystalYes, you are going to see him tomorrow. I see you together.
GirlOh, no, wait a minute. I won't see him tomorrow because he's in Liverpool. I'm not going to see him till Friday.
Dr. CrystalBut I see Jason—with a girl. Perhaps the girl isn't you ... 
GirlOh!
Dr. CrystalOr perhaps that girl is you, but the young man isn't Jason.
GirlHas the young man got dark hair?
Dr. CrystalYes.
GirlAnd is he tall?
Dr. CrystalYes.
GirlThat's David. He asked me if I'd go to the cinema tomorrow.
Dr. CrystalAnd you will go!
GirlYes, I think I will.
Dr. CrystalTomorrow will be an important day.
GirlYes?
Dr. CrystalDavid or Jason?
GirlYes.
Dr. CrystalI see a good future for you.
GirlOh!
Dr. CrystalYou will be married. You will have children. You will be lucky. I see money.
GirlOh, good.
Dr. CrystalOne pound. Come and see me again. I will have more news for you.
GirlYes, I will. Thank you. Good-bye.
Dr. CrystalYes—it's a hard life.

2. Everything is possible in the other world.

LadyI have some questions for Cedric.
Dr. CrystalYes.
LadyHe is in the other world.
Dr. CrystalI see.
LadyMy questions are: can he hear me and see me? Will I see him again?
Dr. CrystalI will ask him. One moment. Yes, Cedric, speak. Yes I hear you. Yes, I will tell her. He said he can hear you and see you all the time. And he asked if you could see him. He is near you.
LadyNo, Cedric, I can't see you. Where are you?
Dr. CrystalHe is there. He said that one day you would see him again.
LadyOh, good. And now the most important question. It's about Tom. Should I say yes or no to him? Will Cedric be angry if he comes into the house?
Dr. CrystalAh! Another man in your life!
LadyNot another man—another dog. Are you sure you're speaking to Cedric? Can you speak to dogs?
Dr. CrystalEverything is possible in the other world. Yes. Yes. Cedric, I understand. He will not be angry. He says you have to take Tom into your house. You need a friend.
LadyOh, thank you, Cedric. Thank you.
Dr. CrystalAnd he says you have to come here again in a few days' time. Come and talk to him.
LadyI will. Thank you, Dr. Crystal.
Dr. CrystalThank you.

3. I wish I could, but I can't.

Dr. CrystalSpeak.
Young manOh, right. Now, Manchester United is playing Leeds on Saturday. Who will win? And who will win at Everton, Ipswich ... 
Dr. CrystalYoung man!
Young manYeah?
Dr. CrystalI will tell you about your future but not about games of football.
Young manOh.
Dr. CrystalI wish I could, but I can't.
Young manOh, all right. Cheerio!
Mrs. CrystalGeorge!
Dr. CrystalWhat's the matter, Doris?
Mrs. CrystalThere's a policeman outside. He asked if you could go and talk to him.
Dr. CrystalI can't see him now.
Mrs. CrystalHe said he'd wait.
Dr. CrystalOh, no!
Mrs. CrystalOh, George! What will happen?
Dr. CrystalI don't know what will happen ... 
Mrs. CrystalWell, look in there ... !

4. I really can see the future.

Dr. CrystalDoris!
Mrs. CrystalYes?
Dr. CrystalI really can see the future.

(In crystal ball: Dr. Crystal in jail.)
