Unit 12

Situational Dialogue 1

ConstableYes, madam. Can I help you?
Mrs. LangleyI'm looking for my daughter.
ConstableYour daughter? What's her name?
Mrs. LangleyAnne. Anne Langley.
ConstableAnne Langley. And how old is she?
Mrs. LangleyShe's seven.
ConstableYes. And what does she look like?
Mrs. LangleyShe's short, about three feet six inches. She's thin, and she's got long dark hair. She's wearing an orange blouse, a green and white skirt and a brown coat.
ConstableIs she carrying anything?
Mrs. LangleyYes. A small black bag and a parcel.
ConstableHello. Yes. A little girl, yes. Short, thin, long dark hair. What is she wearing? An orange blouse, green and white skirt and a brown coat. Yes. Is she carrying anything? A small black bag and a parcel. Good. Yes, yes. Thank you. Goodbye. Your daughter, Mrs. Langley.
Mrs. LangleyOh, thank you.

Situational Dialogue 2

JaneWhat time is it?
LucyIt's half past four. It's all right. Anyway, what does your brother look like?
JaneThat's John (,showing picture).
LucyMm, how old is he?
JaneForty-five.
LucyAnd how tall is he?
JaneHe's not very tall. About five foot six, five foot eight.
LucyWhat color are his eyes?
JaneHe's got blue eyes.
LucyAnd what colour is his hair?
JaneHe's got fair hair.
LucyOh, is that him?
JaneNo.
LucyLook. Is that your brother over there?
JaneNo. That man's not very fair and he's quite tall.
LucyWell. What about that one in the black coat?
JaneNo. John looks younger. Oh, there he is. John! John!
JohnYes. Hello, Jane.
JaneHello, John. John, this is Lucy.
LucyHello.
