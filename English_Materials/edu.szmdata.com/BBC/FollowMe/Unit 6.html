Situational Dialogue 3

PolicemanJust a few questions, Mr. Bird.
Mr. BirdCertainly, certainly.
PolicemanNow, sir. When do the staff arrive?
Mr. BirdAt half past eight every morning.
PolicemanHalf past eight. When do the doors open?
Mr. BirdAt nine o'clock.
PolicemanNine o'clock. Every day, sir?
Mr. BirdEveryday, except Sundays.
PolicemanEveryday except Sunday. And when do the staff leave, sir?
Mr. BirdAt six o'clock.
PolicemanSix o'clock. Every day, sir?
Mr. BirdAt six o'clock on weekdays and one o'clock on Saturdays.
PolicemanI see.
