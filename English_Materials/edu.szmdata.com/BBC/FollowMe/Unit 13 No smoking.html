Unit 13: No smoking

1. May I?

FrancisHello!
Air HostessIs this your bag, sir?
FrancisYes, it is.
Air HostessYou can't put it up there, sir.
FrancisOh. May I put it here?
Air HostessI'm afraid not.
FrancisMay I put it here?
Air HostessYes, of course.
PassengerCan I smoke here?
FrancisYes, I think so.
Air HostessPlease, don't smoke, sir. You can't smoke now, I'm afraid. Look at the sign.
PassengerSorry, I don't understand.
Air HostessYou can't smoke now. Look at the sign. It says "No smoking".
PassengerI'm sorry. I'm very sorry, (to Francis) May I?
