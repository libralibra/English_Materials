Unit 14: It's on the first floor

1. It's on the first floor.

FrancisHello. This is my new office. I work here, on the ground floor. And there are offices on the first floor, the second floor, the third floor, the fourth floor and the fifth floor. First, second, third, fourth, fifth. And I work on the ground floor. Excuse me. Good morning.
AmericanGood morning. I'm looking for Mr. Robinson.
FrancisMr. Robinson's office is on the second floor. Room 205.
AmericanThank you.
Young ManExcuse me. Is there a telephone here?
FrancisYes. It's on this floor, the ground floor. Over there, next to the lift.
Young ManThank you.
FrancisCan I help you?
JaneYes, please. I want to see Mrs. Bramley.
FrancisMrs. Bramley's office is on the fourth floor. Take the lift to the fourth floor. It's Room 403.

Young Man(on the phone) That's right. You're on the fifth floor. The second door on the right. Room 516. I'll be there in a moment.
