Unit 13

Situational Dialogue 3

HusbandI'm hungry. Let's go to a restaurant.
WifeFine. Let's go to Franco's.
HusbandNo, I don't like Franco's .
WifeWe can meet Bob and Jean there. They always go to Franco's on Sunday.
HusbandNo. Let's go to a Chinese restaurant.
WifeAll right.
HusbandCome on then.
 
WifeShall I get you a beer?
HusbandNo, thanks. Would you give me a cup of tea?
WifeOh, yes, of course. Just a moment. Shall we go to the cinema tonight?
HusbandNo. Let's stay at home and watch television.
WifeWe always watch television.
HusbandOh, all right. We'll go to the cinema. But don't you wear your red dress.
WifeShall I wear my black dress?
HusbandYes, I like your black dress.
WifeHere is your cup of tea.
