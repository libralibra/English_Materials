Mash-up

Summary

What happens when we take two already made things and combine them to make something new? Dan and Feifei will clue you in!

Transcript

Dan
Hello and welcome to The English We Speak from BBC Learning English, I'm Dan and with me is Feifei.

Feifei
Hello.What are all these cds and records for Dan?

Dan
I'm trying to make a mash-up. It's going to be so cool.

Feifei
A mash-up? What's that?

Dan
You know, when you combine two or more existing pieces of media to create something new. You get them all the time on YouTube. My mash-up is going to be a song.

Feifei
Oh I see. I think I saw one or two online. Someone had taken a bunch of old movie trailers and mashed them up to create a new one.

Dan
Yes, that's it. They've become very popular recently. And it doesn't have to be a song or movie, people mash-up everything…even pictures and books!

Feifei
Let's hear some examples.
