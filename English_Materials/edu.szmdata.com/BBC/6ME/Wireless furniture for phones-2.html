The future of furniture

Are you tethered to your smartphone? Is it a struggle to keep it charged? The answer may be wireless furniture.

Listen to Rob and Neil discussing battery life, coffee tables, toxic waste and some technology-related vocabulary.

Remember to subscribe to the podcast version!

This week's question:

What do modern phone batteries contain? Is it ... 

  1. nickel
  2. lithium
  3. lead-acid

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

Vocabulary

  • compatible: things that can be used together
  • state-of-the-art: using the latest technology
  • dinosaur: (here) someone or something that is very old-fashioned and no longer useful or effective
  • wireless furniture: furniture with built-in technology which allows you to charge your phone
  • built-in: part of something, and not separate from it
  • wireless technology: modern equipment and machines that can operate without wires
  • I'm not holding my breath: (informal phrase) don't expect something to happen anytime soon
  • functionality: things that a piece of equipment can do
  • think green: consider the environment
  • user-replaceable: allows the user of a gadget to change the battery
  • sealed unit: something that cannot be opened
  • toxic: poisonous
