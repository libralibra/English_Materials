Get on with it

From watching cat videos on YouTube to searching your own name online ... people do all sorts of things in order to delay doing the tasks we really should be doing. Rob and Alice discuss why it can be difficult to get on with things.

This week's question

Which American politician said: "You may delay, but time will not?" Was it …

a) John F Kennedy?

b) Benjamin Franklin?

c) Franklin Roosevelt?

You can hear the right answer at the end of the programme.

Vocabulary

procrastination
delaying doing something you're supposed to do

priority
something that is more important than other things

minimize
reduce

knuckle down
start working hard

tedious
boring

temptation bundling
doing two activities together: one you should do but are avoiding, and one that you love doing but isn't productive

fallacy
a mistaken belief or idea
