How to cure writer's block

Who were the Muses and how did they help the creative process? Neil and Alice discuss Neil's poetry and some interesting ways to deal with writer's block.

This week's question

How does author of the Da Vinci Code, Dan Brown, deal with writer's block? Does he…

a) hang upside down from the ceiling in gravity boots?

b) clean his 6-bedroom house from top to bottom with a toothbrush?

c) run a half-marathon listening to opera music by Richard Wagner?

You'll hear the answer at the end of the programme.
