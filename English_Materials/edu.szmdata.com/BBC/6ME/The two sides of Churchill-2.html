This year marks the 50th anniversary of Winston Churchill's death. He is known throughout the world for his role in defeating Nazi Germany but he also made mistakes. Neil and Mike talk about this historical figure. Listen to their conversation and learn some new vocabulary.

This week's question:

Which of these facts about Winston Churchill is false?

  1. He won the Nobel Peace Prize.
  2. He tried three times before passing exams to the Military Academy.
  3. He was a writer and a painter.

Listen to the programme to find out the answer.

Vocabulary

  • surrender: give up a fight and submit to the enemy
  • controversial: describes something people disagree about
  • determined: persistent, someone who doesn't give up on something easily
  • skate over: avoid
  • misjudged: got the wrong opinion
  • flawed: weak in character
  • strategy: plan of action
  • lacklustre: dull, uninspiring
