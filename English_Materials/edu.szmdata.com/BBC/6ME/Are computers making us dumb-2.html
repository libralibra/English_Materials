We don't leave home without our mobile phones—and are always consulting apps like spellcheckers and maps. Rob and Neil talk about how dependent we are on computers—and wonder if this is a good thing. Listen to their conversation and learn some new vocabulary.

Vocabulary

  • smartphone: phone which allows you to connect to the internet
  • app (application): a computer programme for a specific purpose
  • to connect: to link or join one thing to another thing
  • crash: stop operating (computers and systems)
  • skill: the ability to do something well because you have practised it
  • talent: a natural ability to do something well
  • empathy: the ability to imagine and understand what another person might be feeling
  • GPS: Global Positioning System which gives us information about location and directions with the help of satellites orbiting Earth
  • spellchecker: software which prevents us from making spelling mistakes when we type text on a computer
