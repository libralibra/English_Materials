Praise and admiration

praise noun

an expression of strong approval or admiration

admiration noun

a feeling of respect and approval

recognition noun

praise, respect, or admiration

respect noun

a feeling of admiration that you have for someone because of their personal qualities, their achievements, or their status, and that you show by treating them in a polite and kind way

credit noun

praise for something you have done or achieved

regard noun

respect and admiration for someone or something

compliment noun

something nice that you say to praise someone

acclaim noun

public praise for someone or something

brownie points noun

approval you get from your teacher or boss by doing extra work or special favours

a pat on the back phrase

praise for having done something good
