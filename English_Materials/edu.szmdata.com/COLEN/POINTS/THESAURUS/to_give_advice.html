To give advice

advise verb

to give your opinion to someone about the best thing to do in a particular situation

recommend verb

to advise someone that they should do something

counsel verb

to give someone advice and help with their problems, especially as your job

urge verb

to advise someone very strongly about what action or attitude they should take

admonish verb

to advise someone to do something

guide verb

to help someone to do something by giving them advice at different stages

enjoin someone to do something phrase

to strongly advise or order someone to do something

give someone a steer phrase

to give someone advice or information that helps them to do something

preach verb

to give people advice, or to tell them how to behave, in a way that annoys them

would modal verb

used for advising someone to do something or for suggesting that they do it
