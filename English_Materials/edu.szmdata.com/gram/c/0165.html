1.191 informal use of 'this' and 'these'

In informal spoken English, people sometimes use 'this' and 'these' in front of nouns even when they are mentioning someone or something for the first time.

  • And then this woman came up to me and she said, 'I believe you have a goddaughter called Celia Ravenscroft.'
  • At school we had to wear these awful white cotton hats.
