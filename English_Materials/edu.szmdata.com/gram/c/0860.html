5.60 Adjuncts with future tenses

When you want to make a general or vague reference to future time, you use an adjunct which refers to indefinite time.

  • I'll drop by sometime.
  • Sooner or later he'll ask you to join him there.
  • In future she'll have to take sedentary work of some sort.

Here is a list of adjuncts of indefinite time which are used mainly with future tenses:

  • in future, in the future, one of these days, some day, sometime, sooner or later
