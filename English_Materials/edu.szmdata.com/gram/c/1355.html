10.86 emphasizing exactness

You may want to emphasize that your statement is not only generally true, but that it is true in all its details. The adverbs 'exactly', 'just', and 'precisely' are used for this.

  • They'd always treated her exactly as if she were their own daughter.
  • Their decor was exactly right.
  • I know just how you feel.
  • The peasants are weak precisely because they are poor.
