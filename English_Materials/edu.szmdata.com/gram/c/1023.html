6.124 particular prepositions after nouns and adjectives

Particular prepositions are used after some nouns and adjectives when adding information. See paragraphs 2.49 to 2.54 and 2.304 to 2.307.

  • My respect for her is absolutely enormous.
  • We welcome the Government's emphasis on voluntary codes of practice.
  • ...the solicitor responsible for pursuing the claim.
