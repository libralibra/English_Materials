2.123 used with other adjectives

A superlative is sometimes accompanied by another adjective ending in '-able' or '-ible'. This second adjective can be placed either between the superlative and the noun group or after the noun group.

  • ...the narrowest imaginable range of interests.
  • ...the most beautiful scenery imaginable.
  • ...the longest possible gap.
  • 'Who invited you?' said Etta, in the pleasantest manner possible.
