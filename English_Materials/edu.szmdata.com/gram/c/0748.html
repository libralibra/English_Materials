4.175 requests: 'would like' and 'should like'

You can use 'would like' or 'should like' in a declarative sentence to make a request. 'Would like' and 'should like' are followed by a 'to'-infinitive clause or a noun group.

  • I would like to ask you one question.
  • I'd like to have a little talk with you.
  • I should like a large cutlet, please.
