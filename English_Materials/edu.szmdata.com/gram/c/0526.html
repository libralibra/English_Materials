3.138 WARNING

You cannot use all adjectives with all link verbs. Some verbs, such as 'be' and 'look' are used with a wide range of adjectives and some are used with a restricted range. For example, 'taste' is used only with adjectives which describe the taste of something; 'go' is used primarily with adjectives which indicate colour or madness; and 'fall' is used mainly with 'asleep', 'ill', and 'silent'.

  • Sea water tastes nasty.
  • Its flesh tasted sweet.
  • Jack went red.
  • The world has gone crazy.
  • He fell asleep at the table.
  • The courtroom fell silent.
