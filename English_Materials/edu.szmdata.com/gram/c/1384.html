10.117 titles

When you address someone in a fairly formal way, you use their title and surname. Information about titles is given in 1.56 to 1.58.

  • Goodbye, Dr Kirk.
  • Thank you, Mr Jones.
  • How old are you, Miss Flewin?

Titles indicating a special qualification, rank, or job can be used on their own.

  • What's wrong, Doctor?
  • Well, professor?
