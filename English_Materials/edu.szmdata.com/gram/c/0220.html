2.24 Identifying qualities: qualitative adjectives

There are two main types of adjective, qualitative and classifying. Adjectives that identify a quality that someone or something has, such as 'sad', 'pretty', 'small', 'happy', 'healthy', 'wealthy', and 'wise', are called qualitative adjectives.

  • ...a sad story.
  • ...a pretty girl.
  • ...a small child.
  • ...a happy mother with a healthy baby.
  • ...wealthy bankers.
  • I think it would be wise to give up.
