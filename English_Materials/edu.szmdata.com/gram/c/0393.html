2.266 expressing percentages

Fractions are often given in a special form as a number of hundredths. This type of fraction is called a percentage. For example, 'three hundredths', expressed as a percentage, is 'three per cent'. It can also be written as 'three percent' or '3%'. 'A half' can be expressed as 'fifty per cent', 'fifty percent', or '50%'.

  • 90 percent of most food is water.
  • About 20 per cent of student accountants are women.
  • Before 1960 45% of British trade was with the Commonwealth.

You can use percentages on their own as noun groups when it is clear what they refer to.

  • Ninety per cent were self employed.
  • ...interest at 10% per annum.
