3.176 adjuncts of place

Some verbs which are used with complements can also be used with adjuncts of place after them.

  • She's in California.
  • I'll stay here with the children.
  • The cat was now lying on the sofa.

Here is a list of these verbs:

  • be, keep, remain, stay, --- , hang, lie, sit, stand

For more information about these adjuncts of place, see paragraphs 6.53 to 6.72 and 6.82 to 6.93.
