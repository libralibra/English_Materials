4.39 requests, orders, and instructions

When you are making a request, you usually use a 'yes/no'-question beginning with one of the modals 'could', 'can', or 'would'.

  • Could I ask you a few questions?
  • Can I have my hat back, please?
  • Would you mind having a word with my husband?

You can also give an order or instruction using a 'yes/no'-question beginning with a modal.

  • Will you tell Watson I shall be in a little late?

These uses are explained in detail in paragraphs 4.160 to 4.170.
