2.226 ordinal numbers

When you want to identify or describe something by indicating where it comes in a series or sequence, you use an ordinal number, or an ordinal, such as 'first', 'second', 'fourteenth', or 'twenty-seventh'.

  • She received a video camera for her fourteenth birthday.
  • She repeated this escape for the second and last time.

The use of ordinal numbers is explained in paragraphs 2.249 to 2.256.
