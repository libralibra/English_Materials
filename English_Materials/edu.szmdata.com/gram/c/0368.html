2.239 WARNING

When you use 'dozen', 'hundred', 'thousand', 'million', or 'billion' they remain singular even when the number in front of them is greater than one.

2.240

You can use 'dozen', 'hundred', 'thousand', 'million', or 'billion' without 'of' in a less precise way by putting 'several', 'a few', and 'a couple of' in front of them.

  • ...several hundred people.
  • A few thousand cars have gone.
  • ...life a couple of hundred years ago.
