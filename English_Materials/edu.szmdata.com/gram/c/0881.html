5.86 times of the day

The most frequently used words for periods of the day are 'morning', 'afternoon', 'evening', and 'night'. There are also some words which refer to the rising and setting of the sun, such as 'dusk' and 'sunset', and others which refer to mealtimes.

  • On a warm, cloudy evening, Colin went down to the river.
  • They seem to be working from dawn to dusk.
  • Most of the trouble comes outside the classroom, at break-time and dinner-time.
