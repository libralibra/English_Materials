2.220 USAGE NOTE

You can sometimes use a mass noun instead of a partitive structure. For example, 'two teas' means the same as 'two cups of tea', and 'two sugars' means 'two spoonfuls of sugar'.

  • We drank a couple of beers.
  • I asked for two coffees with milk.

Mass nouns are explained in paragraphs 1.29 to 1.32.
