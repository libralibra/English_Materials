10.60 indicating viewpoint

With adverbs such as 'luckily', 'fortunately', 'happily', and 'unfortunately', you can indicate whose viewpoint you are giving by adding 'for' and a noun group referring to the person.

  • 'Does he do his fair share of the household chores?' --- 'Oh yes, fortunately for me.'
  • Luckily for me and them, love did eventually grow and flourish.
