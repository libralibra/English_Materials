7.86 mentioning the cause of a feeling

If you want to say what causes someone to have a particular feeling, you can mention the cause of the feeling in a 'that'-clause after an adjective describing the feeling.

  • Everybody was sad that she had to return to America.
  • I am confident that I shall be able to persuade them to go.
  • I was worried that she'd say no.

Here is a list of adjectives describing feelings:

  • afraid, angry, anxious, confident, frightened, glad, happy, pleased, proud, sad, sorry, surprised, upset, worried
