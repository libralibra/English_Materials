4.53 position with non-finite verbs

When 'not' is used with an '-ing' form or a 'to'-infinitive clause, it is placed in front of the '-ing' form or 'to'-infinitive.

  • We stood there, not knowing what was expected of us.
  • He lost out by not taking a degree at another university.
  • Try not to worry.
  • It took a vast amount of patience not to strangle him.
