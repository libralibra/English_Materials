1.131 used with personal pronouns

Although you use singular verbs with indefinite pronouns, if you want to use a pronoun to refer back to an indefinite pronoun, you use the plural pronouns 'they', 'them', 'their', or 'themselves'.

  • Ask anyone. They'll tell you.
  • There's no way of telling somebody why they've failed.
  • Everyone put their pens down.
  • No one liked being young then as they do now.
  • Everybody's enjoying themselves.

See paragraph 1.108 for more information about 'they' used to refer to one person.
