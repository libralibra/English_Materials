2.87 classifying '-ed' adjectives

Many other '-ed' adjectives are used for classifying, and so are not gradable. For example, 'a furnished apartment' is one type of apartment, contrasting with 'an unfurnished apartment.'

  • ...a furnished apartment.
  • ...a painted wooden bowl.
  • ...the closed bedroom door.

Most adjectives which refer to physical distinctions are usually classifying adjectives.
