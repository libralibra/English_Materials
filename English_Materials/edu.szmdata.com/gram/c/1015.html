6.116 reason or cause of an action

Prepositional phrases can also be used to say something about the reason for an action, the cause or purpose of it.

  • In 1923, the Prime Minister resigned because of ill health.
  • He was dying of pneumonia.

'As' is used to indicate the status or function of something.

  • He worked as a truck driver.
  • During the war they used the theatre as a warehouse.
