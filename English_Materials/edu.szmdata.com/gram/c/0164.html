1.190 using 'those' instead of 'the'

In more formal English, 'those' can be used instead of 'the' in front of a plural noun when the plural noun is followed by a relative clause. In this use, the relative clause specifies exactly which group of people or things are being referred to.

  • ...those workers who are employed in large enterprises.
  • The parents are not afraid to be firm about those matters that seem important to them.
