2.197 with place names

Some of these quantifiers can also be used with place names.

  • The whole of America will be shocked by what happened.
  • ...involving a large part of Africa and a large part of South America.

Here is a list of quantifiers used with place names:

  • all of, less of, more of, most of, much of, none of, part of, some of, --- , a bit of, a little bit of, a good deal of, a great deal of, a lot of, --- , the rest of, the whole of
