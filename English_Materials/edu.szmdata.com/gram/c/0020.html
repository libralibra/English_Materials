1.20 singular and plural forms

For most count nouns the plural form has '-s' at the end, which distinguishes it from the singular form.

  • ...bed...beds.
  • ...car...cars.

Some count nouns have other differences between the singular and plural forms.

  • ...bus...buses.
  • ...lady...ladies.
  • ...calf...calves.
  • ...man...men.
  • ...mouse...mice.
