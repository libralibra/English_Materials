2.12 structural restrictions

Most adjectives can be used in attributive and predicative structures. However, there are some which can be used only in one or the other. This is explained in paragraphs 2.45 to 2.57.

2.13

There are a small number of adjectives that can be used after the noun rather than attributively or predicatively. They are explained in paragraphs 2.62 to 2.66.
