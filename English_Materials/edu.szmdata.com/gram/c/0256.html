2.91 '-ed' adjectives with an active meaning

A few '-ed' adjectives are related to the past participle of intransitive verbs and have an active meaning, not a passive meaning. For example, 'a fallen tree' is a tree which has fallen.

  • Here and there a fallen tree-trunk spans the narrow gorge.
  • She is the daughter of a retired army officer.
  • ...an escaped prisoner.

Here is a list of '-ed' adjectives with an active meaning:

  • accumulated, dated, escaped, faded, fallen, retired, swollen, wilted
