2.23 attributive and predicative positions

In the following examples, the first example in each pair shows an adjective being used attributively, while the second example shows it being used predicatively.

  • There was no clear evidence.
  • 'That's very clear,' I said.
  • It had been a pleasant evening.
  • It's not a big stream, but it's very pleasant.
  • She bought a loaf of white bread.
  • The walls were white.
