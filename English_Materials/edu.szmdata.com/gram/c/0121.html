1.136 use of adjectives

You can also use adjectives to add information. Note that adjectives are placed after the indefinite pronoun rather than in front of it, and that you do not use a determiner. You do not say 'an important someone', you say 'someone important'.

  • What was needed was someone practical.
  • They are doing everything possible to take care of you.
  • There is nothing wrong with being popular.
