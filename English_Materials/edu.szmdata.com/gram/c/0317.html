2.178 use of proper names

Proper nouns can also be used as noun modifiers. For example, if you want to show that something is connected with a place, organization, or institution, you put the name of the place, organization, or institution in front of all other noun modifiers. You also put them in front of classifying adjectives.

  • ...Brighton Technical College.
  • ...the Cambridge House Literacy Scheme.

Proper nouns are explained in paragraphs 1.53 to 1.59.
