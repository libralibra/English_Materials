Care in planning and management is needed if a factory is to run well. Work areas should be large, clean, well-lighted, and airy. Work should move in a straight line from one machine to another, allowing materials in different stages of production to advance smoothly. Careful planning and intelligent execution can also contribute to harmonious labor-management relations.
